These stunning blue and black short-bodied fish are sure to make a statement in any home freshwater aquarium. The Blue Polar Parrot Cichlid can be an agressive species during the mating season and is known to be an intermediate level fish, they find tropical temperature waters to be the most relaxing and are known for their overall hardiness. This Cichlid is a fascinating creature that will brings with it unique behavior patterns and vibrant blue, pink and black coloring. Truly a rarity and the perfect fish for any aquarium enthusiast who enjoys a challenge. Don't miss out on the opportunity to add this amazing species to your collection.
