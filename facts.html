About Me!

The pandemic took a tole on us all but I also believe it played a pivatol positive role in all of our lives. We all learned to hold eachother a little closer, love a little harder, and of course wash our hands! Like many I know during the pandemic I found myself with an abundance of time with what seemed like nothing to do, it's then I realized I had been working so much my whole life that I hadn't ever found something that I enjoyed doing. I almost forgot life existed outside of work all together and that's when I began noticing a gravitation towards the aquatic fishkeeping hobby. What started out as a hobby quickly multiplied, no pun intended and now I share my fishes babies all over the world!
